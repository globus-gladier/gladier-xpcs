X-ray Photon Correlation Spectroscopy (XPCS) is an experimental technique used at Argonne's Advanced Photon Source to study dynamics in materials at nanoscale by identifying correlations in time series of area detector images. This process involves analyzing the pixel-by-pixel correlations for different time intervals.
